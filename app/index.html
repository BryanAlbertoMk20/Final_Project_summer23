About Study Zone

Study Zone is a site where students can take notes about any subject and download them as PDF. The goal is to provide a study environment where we promote persistent studying but also a place for relaxation. The more comfortable and relaxed when learning new material the easier it is for someone to absorb what they are writing down.

How It Works

We have a page where the student can write down their notes and then save them to their computer for later while keeping track of how long it takes them to study. We also provide a section on our site that promotes relaxation by providing a calming background and ambient music.
